Terms of Service

Bloom is a free wellness education and tracking platform. By using Bloom you agree that you are 16 or older, the information provided is for educational purposes only and not a substitute for medical advice, and you will consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

Privacy Policy

  • Local storage only: All your health data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.
  • No tracking: We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels.
  • AI data: When using the AI Coach, your message goes directly to OpenAI via your own API key — Bloom never sees it.
  • Account data: Your name, email, and hashed password are stored only in your browser's localStorage.
  • No sharing: We never sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party.

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage.
